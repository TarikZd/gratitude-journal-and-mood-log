## Product Hunt Description
**Tagline**: A calm, formula-powered gratitude journal and mood tracker built in Notion.

**Description**:
Five minutes of daily journaling can change your life. The Gratitude Journal & Mood Log gives you a beautiful, private space in Notion to practice daily gratitude, track your mood over time, and spot emotional patterns — all powered by Notion's Formulas 2.0.

✨ **Features**:
- **Mood Score Badges**: Formula 2.0 auto-converts your 1–10 mood rating to 🌟 Excellent / 😊 Good / 💙 Tough Day badges.
- **Calendar Mood View**: See your emotional journey at a glance on a monthly calendar.
- **Holistic Tracking**: Log sleep, exercise, stress level, energy, and daily wins alongside your gratitudes.
- **Vault Method**: Raw journal database stays hidden — your dashboard shows only what you need.
- **Plug & Play**: Duplicate and write your first entry in under 60 seconds.
